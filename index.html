eXview Edge shares one platform across four sizes: 135″, 162″, 190″ and 216″, each built from the same 600×337.5mm cabinet, so specifying a larger room later means adding cabinets — not relearning a new product. Front-accessible, hot-swappable service and an Android-based control system come standard across the range.
