Data Review Notes

Open items still need factory input. Resolved items are logged here for traceability — each was a discrepancy caught during parsing and confirmed against the factory before this revision.

Open

  • Packing dimensions for 190″ and 216″ are pending. Wooden case and carton packing data exist for 135″/162″ only; the factory has confirmed this is still to be determined for the two largest models.

Resolved during this review

  • 216″ resolution/pixel pitch: confirmed as 1920×1080 at 2.5 mm pitch. This matches the original source document and is consistent with its ≥5 m recommended viewing distance — coarser than the 1.875 mm pitch shared by 162″/190″, not a copy-paste error as first suspected.
  • Net weight for 190″ and 216″: the factory confirmed the originally published totals (235 kg and 305 kg) were flawed engineering estimates. Recalculated using the confirmed 4 kg/cabinet rate: 196 kg (190″, 49 cabinets) and 256 kg (216″, 64 cabinets).
  • RS232 connector: confirmed 3.5 mm jack. The source documents' feature-list wording “RS232(RJ45)” was imprecise — the RJ45 reference is the separate LAN port on the sending card, used for UDP-based remote control via eXconfig, not the RS232 port itself.
  • Certifications: confirmed RoHS, CE, and FCC. Source documents mentioned RoHS only.
  • Cabinet seam: confirmed there is none — the assembled display surface is seamless. The 6 mm bezel is the outer frame only.
  • Installation mode: confirmed no ceiling-mount option is offered. 135″/162″ support Stand/Trolley plus wall-mounting; 190″/216″ are wall-mount only, with no Stand/Trolley accessory available.
  • Refresh rate and scan rate: source documents listed a single shared value (“1920/3840 Hz”) for all four models with no scan rate field. Confirmed per-model figures: 135″ — 7680 Hz refresh, 1/64 scan; 162″ and 190″ — 3840 Hz refresh, 1/40 scan; 216″ — 3840 Hz refresh, 1/27 scan.

Methodology note: cabinet grid, cabinet size, and per-cabinet resolution are not literal fields in the source documents — they are derived from stated display dimensions, pixel pitch, and resolution, then cross-checked against net weight ÷ 4 kg per cabinet for all four models. All four resolve to a consistent 600 × 337.5 mm cabinet with no rounding error.
